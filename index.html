Contact
How to get in touch with me.
I'm a game designer & programmer who's mostly known for participating in a lot of game jams and getting awards in some of them. Most of my games are fangames of Team Shanghai Alice's Touhou Project. You can check them out on my Itch page. I'm fluent in English, and good enough in Japanese to prepare online presentations.

I'm also a generalist who's trying to branch into other creative fields, such as digital illustrations . Recently, I'm also self-studying 3DCG.

Currently, my game engine of choice is the Unity game engine, though I'm always open to trying other options.

For a more technical introduction to my technical abilities, check out my GitHub profile.
